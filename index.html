Method

These are rough comparisons, not audit tables.

Totals are based on official adopted, current, or proposed all-funds city budgets depending on what each city publishes clearly for a given year.

The category mix is hand-grouped and rounded to make different city budget books comparable. Percentages are intentionally directional.

City pages include the source trail and fiscal-year notes needed to interpret each timeline.
